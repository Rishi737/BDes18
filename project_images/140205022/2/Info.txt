Brief : In this project we thoroughly studied about the security workers in Educational Institutions and conceptualized 
on how they can be benifitted from the rich intellect present in the institutions(the students) and through technology. A 
case study of IIT Guwahati was done for this project which employs around 400 security workers at different levels.  

Guide : Prof. Pratul Kalita | Department Of Design, IIT Guwahati
Duration : 4 Months | Jan 2016 - Apr 2016
